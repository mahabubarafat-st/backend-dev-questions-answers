About BackendPro

Learn about our mission to help backend developers succeed in technical interviews

Our Story

BackendPro was created by a team of experienced backend developers who noticed a gap in the resources available for backend interview preparation. While there were plenty of resources for frontend and general coding interviews, backend-specific resources were harder to find.

We started by compiling our own notes and questions from interviews we had conducted and participated in. As we shared these resources with colleagues and friends, we realized there was a significant demand for structured, high-quality backend interview preparation materials.

This led to the creation of BackendPro - a comprehensive course designed specifically for backend developers preparing for technical interviews. Our goal is to provide affordable, accessible, and high-quality resources that help developers succeed in their career journeys.

Today, BackendPro has helped thousands of developers prepare for and succeed in technical interviews at companies of all sizes, from startups to major tech corporations.

Our Mission

Our mission at BackendPro is to empower backend developers with the knowledge and confidence they need to excel in technical interviews and advance their careers.

We believe that access to high-quality educational resources should be affordable and accessible to all developers, regardless of their background or experience level. That's why we've made our course available at a one-time payment of just $5, with free questions available to everyone who registers.

We're committed to continuously improving and expanding our content to ensure it remains relevant and valuable in the ever-evolving landscape of backend development.
